Our Team

We are a dedicated team of researchers from the Department of Computer Systems Engineering at Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology (SLIIT). Our diverse expertise spans machine learning, cybersecurity, data protection, and software development. Together, we're working to address the critical challenges in data loss prevention through innovative applications of artificial intelligence.

Our Institution

This research is conducted at the Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology (SLIIT), one of the leading higher education institutions in Sri Lanka specializing in information technology, engineering, and business management.

The Department of Computer Systems Engineering at SLIIT focuses on innovative research in areas such as cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, computer networks, and embedded systems. Our department is committed to developing cutting-edge solutions to real-world problems through applied research and industry collaboration.

This project is part of our ongoing research initiatives in cybersecurity and data protection, aimed at addressing the growing challenges of data security in the digital age.
